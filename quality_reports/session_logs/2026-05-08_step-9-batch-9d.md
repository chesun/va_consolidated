## Lessons (candidates for MEMORY.md after 9-batch series)

- **Settings.do globals must be enumerated upfront, not inferred per-script.** When relocating a caschls-side batch, grep predecessor `caschls/do/settings.do` for ALL globals that the batch's files reference. Don't assume the 1-2 globals you see at first scan are exhaustive. The `$rawcsvdir` miss cost a Critical deduction.
- **Chain semantics across repointings need cross-script verification.** After repointing renamedata's `$clndtadir/staff/...` writes to CANONICAL, the splitstaff0414 read of the same file must also become CANONICAL. The Tier-1 sandbox-write check passes (no LEGACY writes), but the runtime semantics break (read of stale data). New process rule: **after repointing ANY write to CANONICAL, grep the consolidated tree for reads of the same predecessor LEGACY path; update all of them in the same commit.**
